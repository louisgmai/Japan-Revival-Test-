Our Narrative

Revival Japan is more than a movement; it's a bridge between the deep roots of our heritage and the boundless potential of our future. We believe that by revitalizing local communities through cultural investment, we can create a sustainable path for national growth.

Our mission focuses on three core pillars: Cultural Preservation, Community Empowerment, and Creative Innovation. Through the medium of storytelling and art, we bring these goals to life.

Frequently Asked Questions

The Japan Revival Campaign is a movement to help restore Japan’s spirit, economy, and cultural pride through innovation, heritage, and community-driven initiatives. It combines cultural projects, collector items, and community engagement to inspire unity and renewal.
Japan faces challenges with an aging population, economic stagnation, and cultural traditions at risk of being forgotten. The Japan Revival Campaign addresses these issues by promoting innovation, celebrating heritage, and empowering people and communities to take pride in reviving Japan for future generations.
The campaign is led by One World, a company in Japan, together with trusted partners and community advocates.
The campaign has already gained interest from influential Japanese and global supporters. With the website launch on September 1, 2025, we begin the public awareness phase and invite individuals, companies, and organizations to join as official supporters.
Simply click the “I Support!” button, agree to be listed as a Japan Revival supporter, and allow your name or logo to appear on our supporters page. This shows your public commitment to helping revive Japan.

We encourage artists to collaborate with us by creating Japan Revival art, NFTs, and other works. Selected pieces may be showcased in our official shop, with a portion of each sale dedicated to Japan Revival initiatives.
A portion of proceeds from collector items and campaigns will be donated to select initiatives aligned with Japan Revival’s mission, helping amplify the work of organizations and businesses already dedicated to cultural preservation and community development.
The campaign is not currently accepting direct donations. However, by purchasing collector items — such as the 24kt plated Japan Revival Coin, REVIVAL Manga, and upcoming limited editions — you directly contribute, as a portion of proceeds supports Japan Revival initiatives. The number of collector items will expand over time, offering new ways to contribute and own meaningful pieces of the Revival story.
Collector items can be purchased using major credit cards as well as select digital payment options.
We welcome new voices and ideas. Please contact us directly through the “Get Involved” form on the website to explore opportunities to participate in shaping the Revival.
